## ðŸ“… Week 1: Core Trie Mastery

### Day 1-2: Foundation (3 hours)

**Goal:** Master basic Trie implementation

| Problem | Difficulty | Time | Focus |
|---------|------------|------|-------|
| [LC 208: Implement Trie](https://leetcode.com/problems/implement-trie-prefix-tree/) | Medium | 30 min | Template |
| [LC 14: Longest Common Prefix](https://leetcode.com/problems/longest-common-prefix/) | Easy | 20 min | Prefix concept |
| [LC 720: Longest Word in Dictionary](https://leetcode.com/problems/longest-word-in-dictionary/) | Medium | 30 min | Build incrementally |

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Can implement Trie in 3 minutes
- [ ] Know difference between search() and startsWith()
- [ ] Understand HashMap vs Array[26]

---

### Day 3-4: Wildcard Pattern (2.5 hours)

**Goal:** Handle '.' wildcard with DFS

| Problem | Difficulty | Time | Focus |
|---------|------------|------|-------|
| [LC 211: Add and Search Words](https://leetcode.com/problems/design-add-and-search-words-data-structure/) | Medium | 35 min | DFS for '.' |
| [LC 676: Implement Magic Dictionary](https://leetcode.com/problems/implement-magic-dictionary/) | Medium | 30 min | Exactly one diff |

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Can write recursive DFS through Trie
- [ ] Handle wildcard by trying all children
- [ ] Know time complexity with wildcards

---

### Day 5-6: Trie + Backtracking (2.5 hours)

**Goal:** Combine Trie with grid/word problems

| Problem | Difficulty | Time | Focus |
|---------|------------|------|-------|
| [LC 212: Word Search II](https://leetcode.com/problems/word-search-ii/) | Hard | 45 min | Grid + Trie |
| [LC 79: Word Search](https://leetcode.com/problems/word-search/) | Medium | 30 min | Single word (compare) |

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Use Trie to guide backtracking
- [ ] Store word at end node for retrieval
- [ ] Mark found words to avoid duplicates
- [ ] Understand pruning benefits

---

### Day 7: Review & Consolidation (1 hour)

**Activities:**
- [ ] Re-solve LC 208 without looking (5 min)
- [ ] Re-solve LC 211 without looking (10 min)
- [ ] Re-solve LC 212 without looking (20 min)
- [ ] Write Trie from memory (10 min)
- [ ] Explain patterns out loud (15 min)

---

## ðŸ“… Week 2: Advanced Patterns

### Day 8-9: Autocomplete & Collection (2 hours)

**Goal:** Collect words with prefix

| Problem | Difficulty | Time | Focus |
|---------|------------|------|-------|
| [LC 1268: Search Suggestions System](https://leetcode.com/problems/search-suggestions-system/) | Medium | 35 min | Top-k with prefix |
| [LC 642: Design Search Autocomplete](https://leetcode.com/problems/design-search-autocomplete-system/) | Hard | 45 min | Frequency ranking |

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Store suggestions at each node (trade-off)
- [ ] Or DFS to collect then sort
- [ ] Handle incremental input

---

### Day 10-11: Bit Trie (2.5 hours)

**Goal:** Master XOR problems with Bit Trie

| Problem | Difficulty | Time | Focus |
|---------|------------|------|-------|
| [LC 421: Maximum XOR of Two Numbers](https://leetcode.com/problems/maximum-xor-of-two-numbers-in-an-array/) | Medium | 35 min | Core Bit Trie |
| [LC 1707: Max XOR With Element](https://leetcode.com/problems/maximum-xor-with-an-element-from-array/) | Hard | 45 min | Offline processing |

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Process bits MSB to LSB
- [ ] Greedy opposite bit selection
- [ ] Handle constraints with sorting

---

### Day 12-13: Mixed Practice (1.5 hours)

**Goal:** Apply patterns to new problems

| Problem | Difficulty | Time | Focus |
|---------|------------|------|-------|
| [LC 648: Replace Words](https://leetcode.com/problems/replace-words/) | Medium | 25 min | Shortest prefix |
| [LC 820: Short Encoding of Words](https://leetcode.com/problems/short-encoding-of-words/) | Medium | 25 min | Suffix Trie |

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Recognize when to use Trie
- [ ] Adapt template to problem
- [ ] Analyze complexity correctly

---

### Day 14: Final Review (1 hour)

**Mock Interview:**
- [ ] Pick random problem from list
- [ ] Solve in 25 minutes (interview time)
- [ ] Explain approach out loud
- [ ] Analyze complexity
- [ ] Discuss trade-offs

**Self-Assessment:**
- [ ] Can I implement Trie in < 3 min?
- [ ] Do I know when to use Trie vs HashMap?
- [ ] Can I handle wildcards with DFS?
- [ ] Do I understand Bit Trie for XOR?
- [ ] Can I combine Trie with backtracking?
